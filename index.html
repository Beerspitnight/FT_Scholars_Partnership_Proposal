Meet Our Tier 1 Launch Partners

Two exceptional organizations with proven track records of transforming student lives in New Jersey.

NJ LEEP

Law and Education Empowerment Project

Empowers first-generation and low-income students from the greater Newark area, guiding them from middle school through college graduation with a focus on skills, habits, and exposure.

Strategic Value: An Opportunity to partner with a proven organization that supports FT Scholars student profile.

SEEDS

Access Changes Everything

Prepares and places motivated, high-achieving students from low-income households into elite selective schools and universities with substantial financial aid.

Strategic Value: Prestigious brand alignment, positioning the FT as an essential tool for elite academic environments.
